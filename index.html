Who Are We?
Dedshana is an e-commerce apparel company. We make the most out of the e in our e-commerce. How you ask me? Let me show you. Dedshana aims to break the mould and rattle people out of their comfort for settling for the same, copy pasted fashion. We believe every individual is a born designer with a unique imagination and to celebrate this unique quality of people we decided to enable them to bring their ideas to life. We have created a customized apparel designer tool through which anyone can design apparel as per his/her whims & fancies. Want a superhero logo? Done! Want a funky quote from your favourite web series? Done! Want a famous dialogue from your favourite movie?
YES! YOU CAN DO IT ALL. Here at Dedshana.
Why are we called Dedshana?
It started with 3 engineers, all super busy in their work, running from classes to assignments to submissions with no end at sight. Stuck in a rut, they were helpless with society dangling it's chains and restraining them from spreading their wings too far. Soon they realised that they were forced to do the same monotonous things in their 9-5 job which they never intended to do. Yet deep down they yearned for something more, something that would make them feel alive, something after which they could get behind with their whole and soul. Something which defied society's expectations of them and by which they could truly feel free from its grasp.
They started slowly and under the radar. They started a small apparel enterprise yet they kept it lowkey as they didn't want to attract attention from their friends and families who they feared might judge them for sacrificing the stability of a day to day job to pursue something as reckless and risky as starting a new business. These friends jokingly named the company as "Dedshana". As time passed they realised that life is too short to live by other's rules. So after a lot of planning, hardships, meetings, brainstorming sessions and numerous hours of roaming the streets of Mumbai, that fake venture turned into a real one. Yet, to honour their humble beginnings and intent they decided to stick with the old name, and thus Dedshana was born.
